5. Spaghetti<\/b><\/h3>\n

A thermic lance is a heavy-duty demolition tool that burns steel alloys and aluminum in pressurized oxygen to create temperatures hot enough to cut\u2026 anything, really. Instead of burning steel, this do-it-yourself version uses spaghetti as fuel.<\/p>\n

Spaghetti may not seem particularly flammable, but with pure oxygen just about everything becomes a pyromaniac\u2019s dream. With just an oxygen tank, aluminum foil, and a small handful of spaghetti, you can make a thermic lance that burns hot enough to melt through concrete.<\/p>\n
