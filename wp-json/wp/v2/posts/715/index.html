Indoctrination<\/strong><\/h3>\n

For the first time in our history, we are proud to announce that Blasted Science has adopted a new human.<\/p>\n

After hours of intense research, debate, and meditation, the Blasted Science Board of Executives finally approved the entry of Sam Henry<\/em> into our elite organization as an unpaid intern. Sam is a world-renowned master of videography. This kid has plenty of camera equipment that should speed up our production process.<\/p>\n

Sam’s best quality is his undying love for the Motherland. As a direct descendant of Vladimir Lenin, Sam’s lifelong devotion to communism has been proven time and time again. On a good day, he can seize a country’s means of production in less than 3.75 seconds.<\/p>\n
