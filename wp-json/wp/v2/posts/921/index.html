Where did we go?<\/h1>\n

It’s been a long time since Blasted Science has posted any new content. Why is that?<\/p>\n

Long story short, the group unofficially disbanded after high school when we each went our separate ways. Some of us went to college while others served missions for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.<\/a><\/p>\n
